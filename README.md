# n8n-Sync-Shopify-and-WooCommerce-sales-into-a-Google-Sheets-accounting-ledger
Keep your sales records accounting ready with this **n8n workflow automation**. It captures Shopify and WooCommerce orders in real time, standardizes sales and refunds into a unified format, and syncs every transaction to Google Sheets creating a reliable ledger for finance, reporting, and reconciliation with **n8n**.
